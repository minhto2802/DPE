Summary

Subpopulation shifts can degrade model performance in underrepresented groups. We propose a simple yet effective approach ‚Äî Diversified Prototypical Ensembles (DPE) ‚Äî that replaces the classifier head with multiple prototypes optimized for diversity. Our method improves worst-group accuracy on 9 real-world datasets, outperforming state-of-the-art methods without relying on subgroup annotations.
